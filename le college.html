more about l.e. college


Lukhdhirji Engineering College , popularly known as L.E. college or LEC morbi is located in the morbi distric .Lukhdhirji Engineering College, Morbi, formerly known as Morbi Technical Institute (MIT) was established in 1931 by the Commissionerate of Technical Education (CTE), the Government of Gujarat. The campus of the college spreads over an area of 40 acres of land. Lukhdhirji Engineering College, Morbi is affiliated to Gujarat Technological University. The college offers Diploma, B.E/B.Tech and M.E programmes. The programmes are approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).
