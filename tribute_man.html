A. P. J. Abdul kalam

1931-2015

Missile Man of India


He was known as the 'Missile Man of India' for his work in the development of ballistic missile technology. From 1992 to 1999, Kalam was the Chief Scientific Adviser to the Prime Minister and the Secretary of the DRDO. Kalam was the Chief Project Coordinator during the Pokhran II tests. Between the 1970s and 1990s, Kalam made an effort to develop the Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle (PSLV) and SLV-III projects, both of which proved to be successful. In the 1970s he also directed two projects which developed the ballistic missiles from the technology of the SLV programme.
